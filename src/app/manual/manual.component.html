Space Points

This game is a solo drafting game: every turn you buy a card and gain its effects. You try to get as many points as possible after {{ INITIAL_MAX_HEALTH }} turns!

Screen

At the top of the screen you can see the current status of the game:

  • The turn counter
  • The number of resources you have (money, flags, etc)
  • The amount of weapons you have (small lasers, big lasers)

In the middle of the screen you see a list of cards from which you can buy one every turn. At the bottom of the screen you can see the milestones that are active during the game. Milestones are red, until you achieved it.

Turn

Every turn you are given a choice of {{ INITIAL_NR_OF_CARDS_PER_DRAFT + 1 }} cards. One of those cards is always "Fire lasers". This card is free and increases your flags and money by as much as you have big lasers and small lasers. The other three cards can have any kind of effect. See below for a full list of all the cards.

Milestones

Every game, a random set of milestones are selected. Each milestone always has the same concept: you have to satisfy certain requirements. If you satisfy the requirements at the end of the turn, you gain the milestone and are awarded a number of resources. You can gain as many milestones per turn. These are the full list of milestones:
