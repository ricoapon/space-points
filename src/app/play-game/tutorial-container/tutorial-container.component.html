Welcome to Space Points

Your ship is under attack! Try to fight off as many enemies as you can. Your goal is to kill as many flagships as possible.

Every turn enemies attack you and you will lose 1 health per turn. If it reaches 0, the game is done.

= 0 => Game done

More = Better

Cards

Every turn you can buy a card from a randomized set of cards. Cards cost money.

The card "Fire lasers" is free and is always available. If you pick this card, your small lasers will shoot down ships, which you loot for money. Your big lasers will shoot down flagships.

Milestones

Every game a randomized set of milestones are available. If you satisfy the given criteria, you gain 10 flags.

Each game will have three milestones. If you play smart, you can get all of them for maximal points!

25 / 30 => 0

30 / 30 => 10

Good luck!

That's the basics. As you start to play you'll learn strategies around picking the best cards for your current situation. If you need the tutorial again, click the menu icon in the top right. Good luck and have fun.
