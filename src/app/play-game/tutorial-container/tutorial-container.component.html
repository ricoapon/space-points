Welcome to Space Points

Your ship is under attack! Try to fight off as many enemies as you can. Your goal is to kill as many flagships as possible.

Every turn enemies attack you and you will lose 1 health per turn. If it reaches 0, the game is done.

= 0 => Game done

More = Better

Cards

Every turn you can buy a card from a randomized set of cards. Cards cost money.

The card "Fire lasers" is free and is always available. If you pick this card, your small lasers will shoot down ships, which you loot for money. Your big lasers will shoot down flagships.

Dashboard

On the top of the screen you have the dashboard, which shows you information about the game. Every block indicates how many of that resource are available or how many times you played a card.

20 => You have 20 health

7 => You have 7 money

60 => You have 60 small lasers

Full screen

The game can be played on mobile and PC. It is highly advised to enable full screen when playing this game on mobile. This will make it easier to see all the elements on the screen. You can enable this in the menu.
